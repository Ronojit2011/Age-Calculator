A sleek and user-friendly age calculator tool. Users can input their date of birth to instantly see their age calculated in years, months, and days. The application features a modern, dark-themed UI and is fully responsive for all devices.

== Description ==

This plugin provides a comprehensive and user-friendly **Responsive Age Calculator** via a simple shortcode.

At its core, it's a tool that lets you calculate your age with precision, breaking it down into years, months, and days. It also offers:

*   **Flexible Date Calculation:** Calculate an age not just for today, but for any specific date in the past or future.
*   **Detailed Time Summary:** Once you calculate an age, the app provides a detailed summary, showing the total time lived in months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.
*   **Next Birthday Countdown:** It tells you the exact day of the week your next birthday will fall on and provides a countdown in months and days.
*   **Modern & Responsive UI/UX:** The application is fully responsive, looking and working great on any device. It also includes a sleek dark mode.

To use, simply add the `[responsive_age_calculator]` shortcode to any page, post, or widget.

== Installation ==

1.  Upload the `responsive-age-calculator` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2.  Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  Add the shortcode `[responsive_age_calculator]` to any page or post where you want the calculator to appear.
